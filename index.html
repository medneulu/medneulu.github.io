THE PROJECT IN MY MIND

I plan to develop a strategy and simulation game. The game will take place in Ragako Village, which is in a fantasy universe.
In this game you'll own a plot and the tiny house in that area. The game will focus on the Scandinavian mythology.
In this mythology there are two different divine areas which called Nifleheim and Muspell. Our main character's, the player's, name will be Embla.
The first woman created. Embla is wronged and killed by the gods. Because of her anger and greed for revenge, her soul cannot rest in peace.
In short, this will be the theme of the game.

My game project's first goal is reaching to the people who wants to learn Scandinavian mythology. The second goal is presenting
